# Privacy Policy — Reset

_Last updated: July 23, 2026_

**Reset** ("the app") is a habit-tracking app for Android and iOS, published
by Jheremiah (jheremiah.dev@gmail.com).

## The short version

Reset collects **no data**. Everything you enter stays on your device.

## What data the app handles

- Habits, progress entries, plans, and settings you create are stored in a
  local database **on your device only**.
- The app has **no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no
  tracking** of any kind.
- The app makes **no network requests**. Your data is never transmitted to
  the developer or to any third party.

## Data you export

The app includes an "Export data" feature that copies your data to your
device's clipboard as JSON. What you do with that export is entirely under
your control.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules **local notifications** on your
device. Notification scheduling does not involve any server.

## Health data

Reset does not read or write any health data (such as step counts from
Apple Health or Health Connect). Steps are entered manually. If a future
version adds an optional health integration, it will ask for your permission
first and this policy will be updated before release.

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the app deletes all of its data. You can also erase everything
from within the app via **Profile → Reset app data**.

## Children

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with
a new "Last updated" date.
